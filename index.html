What Is Stable Coin?

Stablecoins are a type of cryptocurrency that are designed to have a stable value,
typically by being pegged to a relatively stable asset or a basket of assets.
The goal of stablecoins is to reduce the volatility that is commonly associated
with many other cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.
This stability makes stablecoins more suitable for use in various applications, including
as a medium of exchange, a store of value, and a unit of account.
